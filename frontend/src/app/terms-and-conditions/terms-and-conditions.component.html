Rewards Policy

When purchasing a room from LikeHome, you will be given reward points. Reward points are determined by the rate per night of the selected room. Users earn 1 reward point per dollar spent, with points calculated based on the ceiling of the rate multiplied by the number of nights. Choosing to earn reward points makes the booking non-modifiable and non-cancelable, while opting out allows flexibility but results in no points. Points can be redeemed at a rate of 5 points per dollar; if a booking is modified or canceled, points are not refunded, but users only pay or receive the difference in the transaction amount. In checkout, make sure to check the “Earn Rewards” box in order to receive rewards. Rates per night are subject to change.

How do rewards points work?

The number of points is determined by the rate and the number of nights. During the payment process, you will be shown the amount of reward points that will be awarded when opting into earning rewards for that booking. Reward points are awarded once the payment has been completed for the current booking. Reward points available will be displayed during the payment process and on the “Account” page.

How do I use my reward points?

When opting in, reward points are awarded to users to redeem free stays at a hotel. Rewards are eligible for use when the number of rewards points is at least 5x the rate of one night. Points are only redeemable to cover full nights. You may pay for your stay with both rewards points and a payment method as long as the reward points have covered at least a full night. Partial night redemptions are not accepted.

Cancellation Policy

How do I cancel my hotel?

Cancellations are available in the “Account” page. Canceling a booking is done with the “Cancel Booking” button. You must re-enter payment details before canceling a booking. You will receive a confirmation of your cancellation. If you cancel within one week of check-in, a 40% cancellation fee will apply. Bookings made using reward points cannot be canceled, and reward points used in the booking are forfeited.

Modification Policy

Can I modify my bookings?

Modifying a booking is done in the “Account” page. You must click the “See Details” link, and the information of your hotel will be alongside your current room. You may modify your Check-In/Check-Out dates. You will need to enter a new payment method in order to receive a partial refund or pay for additional nights. If the modified number of nights is less than your previous booking, you will receive a refund. Additional payment is required if the number of nights exceeds the original booking. A modification fee of $100 is charged if the new or old check-in date is within one week of the booking. Bookings made with reward points cannot be modified, and reward points used in the booking are not refunded.
